# Site Content Section Design

## Goal

Refresh the "personal knowledge base" section so it feels like a product surface instead of a generic image-and-copy block.

## Direction

- Use a light, technical knowledge-panel style.
- Keep the site on a bright background instead of switching to a dark AI aesthetic.
- Increase hierarchy and contrast between editorial copy and product visualization.

## Layout

- Left column: badge, headline, supporting paragraphs, and a stronger quote card.
- Right column: a layered knowledge panel with a preview surface and floating signal cards.
- Background: subtle grid, soft gradients, and thin borders.

## Content Rules

- Keep the existing headline and body copy.
- Add a small set of concise product signals for the right-side panel.
- Preserve bilingual support through the site dictionary.
